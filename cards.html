QUESTIONS

What you might be wondering.

Do we need to download an app?

The cards are browser-based. They work on any phone, tablet, or laptop. No download required.

Is this a subscription?

One-time payment. Permanent access. No recurring charges.

Can I use these alone?

Yes. Many people use the cards for solo reflection, journalling, or preparing for a conversation. The method works one person at a time too.

What is the difference between the decks?

Couples is for romantic relationships, intimacy, touch, desire, repair. Friends & Family covers life stories, beliefs, and connection across all relationships. Trust & Repair is specifically for rupture, the conversation you have been putting off.

Yes. You can access individual decks or upgrade to a bundle at any point.
