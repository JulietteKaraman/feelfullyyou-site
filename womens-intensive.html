What happens in the session

Not a protocol. A conversation between me and your body.

I bring everything I have. EMDR, hypnotherapy, somatic experiencing, spinal attunement, and Rapid Release Rewire Restore®, and I follow what your body needs. Nothing is forced. Everything moves at the pace of your body actually opening.

There is warmth when you arrive. Something that smells like calm. A space that is not asking anything of you yet. And then, almost before you notice it, your shoulders drop.

Arrive

We don't rush to begin. What looks like stillness is your body starting to co-regulate, beginning to remember what its natural state actually feels like. Not forced. Not performed. Just the slow recognition that you are safe, and you can put it all down. That is where the day starts.

Map your pleasure

Using Touch Languages™ and your personal pleasure languages, we discover exactly how your body is wired, which touches open you, which textures, pressures, rhythms, and areas make you come alive. Every woman is different. Most have never been shown their own map. This is the part of the day where you find out who you actually are.

Release what blocks it

Where the body braces, guards, or goes numb, we move it. Not by talking. By going directly to where it lives. And I leave enough space. If something intense surfaces, an old memory, an unexpected wave of feeling, there is room for it. We move it through and out. Nothing gets left in the middle.

Leave with a library

You go home with a full personal touch library, a map of how you are wired, plus a curated set of touches to explore on your own. So the discovery doesn't stop when the day does.

Frequently asked

What is the difference between online and in-person?

In-person is a six-hour immersion in East Sussex, the pace slows significantly and you are held fully in the room. Online is 2.5 hours via Zoom, designed to honour your capacity for sensation. Both formats meet you where you are.

Is this therapy?

No. I bring everything I have. EMDR, hypnotherapy, somatic experiencing, spinal attunement, and my own method Rapid Release Rewire Restore®, to move what is held in the body. Think of it this way: we use whatever the moment needs, not a fixed protocol. You will not spend the day talking about your past. You will spend it actually shifting it.

Do you work with trauma?

Yes. Much of this work involves the quiet aftermath of trauma, particularly where it impacts intimacy, the capacity to receive, and the ability to trust the body. I meet the body where it holds things, without needing words for it.

Is this available outside the UK?

Online is available globally. For in-person sessions outside East Sussex, reach out and we will explore options, additional costs apply.

What happens after the intensive?

You leave with the Intimacy and Communication Cards. Many women also continue with the Six-Week Intensive or The Beginning couples programme.

Is this confidential?

Everything is held in complete confidence. Always.

What is your refund policy?

This is bespoke, personal work. All sales are final. Book when your yes is clear.
