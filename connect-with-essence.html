Photos by the fabulous Justine Stoddart

This isn't just a retreat

It's a recalibration.

Most women are trying to think their way into feeling better. And they're very good at it. They can name what's off, explain it clearly, hold space for everyone else's version of it, and still come home to a body that hasn't quite landed.

At some point you took on an identity. The capable one. The strong one. The one who holds it all together. It served you. It may still serve you. But a new beginning asks a different question: what do you need to put down?

Essence is where that starts. Not with more thinking. With the body.

NEW BEGINNINGS

There are moments where you begin to sense that something is shifting. A quiet feeling that you are ready for more. Or a growing awareness that the way you are currently living, working, or showing up no longer fully fits the version of yourself you are becoming.

What stands between where you are and where you know you could be is rarely a lack of ability. It is the invisible weight you are still carrying. The beliefs inherited over years. The emotional patterns that quietly shape your decisions. The physical exhaustion you have normalised. The stories about what is possible for you.

Too often, women learn to become smaller versions of themselves without ever consciously realising it.

New beginnings rarely start with changing our circumstances. More often, they begin when we give ourselves permission to become a different version of ourselves.

How the day works

One day. Four layers.

Body

Release the tension you have normalised.

Emotion

Meet the patterns that quietly shape your decisions.

Soul

Slow down until you can hear yourself again.

Financial confidence

Reset your relationship with money, worth and independence.

Whether that next chapter is in your personal life, your career, your business or simply the relationship you have with yourself, the intention is the same: to leave with greater clarity, renewed confidence and a genuine sense of possibility.

Am I fully being the woman I know I am capable of? Or am I still becoming her?

Because when women fully step into their own potential, the ripple effect extends far beyond themselves.

The journey

What you'll explore throughout the day

Throughout the day, we'll gently guide you through a series of conversations, reflections and experiences designed to help you pause, reconnect and gain clarity around what comes next.

Together we'll explore

  • Understanding where you are today and where you feel called next.
  • Recognising the beliefs, habits and emotional patterns that may be quietly holding you back.
  • Reconnecting with your body, your intuition and your sense of purpose.
  • Exploring confidence, identity and what it means to step into the next version of yourself.
  • Reflecting on your relationship with money, independence and personal power.
  • Leaving with practical insights, renewed clarity and a stronger vision for your next chapter.

What actually happens

01

Juliette

Patterns & Emotions

An honest look at where you are. Where life feels heavy, where you've been on autopilot, what you've been carrying without naming it.

Then the emotional patterns underneath: fear, old identity, the protective behaviours that got you here but no longer fit.

And then: physical movement. Spinal attunement. Embodied release. The body participates in the shift, not just the mind.

02

Natasha

Soul & Intuition

Meditation. Music. Breathwork. Natasha slows everything down until you can hear yourself again.

Then she takes you into your Akashic Records: the patterns carried across this lifetime and others, surfacing so you can see them clearly and choose differently. Remembrance, not escape.

03

Liz

Your Hormonal Blueprint

Your hormones are not a problem to manage. They are a language your body speaks. Liz helps you learn to read it.

How your cycle shapes your energy, mood, and capacity. What your body is actually telling you, and how to work with it rather than override it. The physical layer beneath everything you've been pushing through.

04

Amy

Money, Worth & Independence

A conversation with Amy Knight of Women Who Scale on financial confidence, independence and the relationship many women have with money.

How beliefs around wealth, worth and opportunity quietly shape the decisions we make and the futures we create. Because a new chapter is easier to step into when you feel financially confident about it.

The woman who creates your next chapter is unlikely to be exactly the same woman who created the last one.
