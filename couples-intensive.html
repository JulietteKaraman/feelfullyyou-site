You love each other. You have just forgotten how to feel it in the room together.

What is happening

The dynamic is off.
The safety is uncertain.

It is not that you stopped caring. It is that somewhere along the way, reaching became something you prepared for instead of something that just happened.

He reaches and she stiffens slightly, not dramatically, just enough. She waits for him to notice and pull back. He does. The distance closes in small degrees until it feels like the distance has always been there.

Both of you still want the same thing. You have just lost the felt sense of how to get there together.

This isn't about scripts or techniques. It is about your bodies learning to trust each other again, in the room, in real time, in silence as much as in touch.
What happens in the room

I meet you both, individually and as a unit.

I bring everything I have. EMDR, hypnotherapy, somatic experiencing, spinal attunement, and Rapid Release Rewire Restore®, to move what is held in the body. We use whatever the moment needs, not a fixed protocol.

Unwind

We surface what has gone unspoken, the unmet needs, the longing that stopped reaching, the pattern neither of you chose.

Map the body

Using Touch Languages™ and pleasure languages, I map one partner's body for the other, exactly which touches land, which textures, pressures, and rhythms open rather than close. Not guesswork. A real map, built in the session, given to the partner who will use it.

Learn to touch

I teach one partner how to touch the other in the way that actually lands, specifically, in the language their body understands. So touch stops feeling like a risk and starts feeling like an arrival.

Leave with a practice

You go home with a personal touch library built around her pleasure languages, and the Intimacy and Communication Cards, so what opened in the room keeps opening between you.

Frequently asked

What is the difference between online and in-person?

In-person is a six-hour immersion in East Sussex, the pace slows significantly and the field is fully held in the room together. Online is 2.5 hours via Zoom, designed to honour your body's capacity. Both formats are equally effective at meeting you where you are.

Is this therapy?

No. I bring everything I have. EMDR, hypnotherapy, somatic experiencing, spinal attunement, and my own method Rapid Release Rewire Restore®, to move what is held in the body. Think of it this way: we use whatever the moment needs, not a fixed protocol. You will not spend the day talking about your past. You will spend it actually shifting it.

Can we do this outside the UK?

Online is available globally. For international in-person sessions, reach out and we will explore bespoke arrangements, additional costs apply.

Do you work with trauma?

Yes. Much of this work involves the quiet aftermath of trauma, particularly where it impacts intimacy, trust, and the capacity to receive. I meet the body where it holds things, without needing words for it.

What happens after the intensive?

You leave with the Intimacy and Communication Cards, a daily structure that keeps what shifted, moving. Many couples also continue with The Beginning, my eight-week couples programme.

Is this confidential?

Everything is held in complete confidence. Always.

What is your refund policy?

This is bespoke, personal work. All sales are final. Book when your yes is clear.
