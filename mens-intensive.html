Frequently asked

Does my partner need to be there?

No. This is work on you. The day is entirely yours, your body, your presence, your recalibration. The shift in her comes from the shift in you.

Is this therapy?

No. I bring everything I have. EMDR, hypnotherapy, somatic experiencing, spinal attunement, and Rapid Release Rewire Restore®, to work directly with what is held in your body. This isn't about processing your past. It's about changing how you show up now.

How is this different from coaching?

Coaching works with the mind. This works with the body. Insight alone doesn't change intimacy, embodied change does. You won't leave with a framework to implement. You'll leave different.

What if something intense comes up?

There is room for it. I leave enough space in every session that if something unexpected surfaces, we move it through and out. Nothing gets left in the middle.

What is the difference between online and in-person?

In-person is six hours in East Sussex, the full immersion, the full mapping, nourishing lunch included. Online is 2.5 hours via Zoom, guiding you into presence through sensation and precision. Both create real, lasting change.

Is this available outside the UK?

Online is available globally. In-person outside East Sussex is available on request, additional costs apply.

What is your refund policy?

This is bespoke, personal work. All sales are final. Book when your yes is clear.
