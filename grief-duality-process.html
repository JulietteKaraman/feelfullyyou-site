The Grief Duality Process
"This isn't a bypass. It's a return."

Grief moves through the body long after we think we've moved on. It lives in the jaw, the chest, the held breath before sleep. It isn't always loud. Sometimes it's the absence of feeling. The numbness. The vague weight that follows you into rooms.

This process doesn't ask you to talk about it. It asks your body to lead.

Woven into Juliette's Rapid Release Rewire & Restore Method®, this 25-minute guided release has helped hundreds of clients soften grief that had nowhere else to go.

Why Juliette created this

She learned it at the threshold.

Juliette has held three of her own family members as they passed. She has sat with clients and their families in the weeks before dying, not as a bystander, but as a presence.

She has worked with those grieving children lost to suicide. With a woman finally releasing the weight of her great-grandmother's death, carried two centuries later through the body.

This process was born from all of it. From the grief that words couldn't touch, and the realisation that the body already knows how to let go, when it's held safely enough to try.

Unexplained heaviness with no clear origin

The grief of witnessing, a parent, a child, a partner
