The situation is where you are. The pattern is how your body learned to protect itself inside it. These are the five Touch Languages patterns. Read them, and watch one describe you a little too well.

The Holder
Give to stay connected

You reach first, you hold everyone, and quietly wonder why no one ever thinks to hold you.

With touch: you give easily and receive awkwardly, bracing the second a hand lands without you earning it.

"I want to be held without having to earn it."

The Flame
Dim to stay digestible

There is fire in you. You learned to turn it down to stay wanted, and now you are quietly starving to be met at full heat.

With touch: you only let it in once you have dimmed for it, so it reaches the softened version of you, never the full heat.

"I want to be wanted without shrinking."

The Armour
Contain to stay safe

You stay composed and in control, because softness once cost you too much. You would rather hold than be held.

With touch: you tense a beat before contact, and would rather give on your terms than be caught receiving.

"I want to soften without collapsing."

The Signal
Curate to stay legible

You stay readable. You translate and filter yourself so well that no one ever meets the raw, unedited you.

With touch: yours is graceful and curated, and you disconnect a beat before it can sink in.

"I want to be met in the full mess of me."

The Current
Express to stay met

You bring the aliveness, you keep the charge even when no one meets you there, and being the source is quietly exhausting you.

With touch: you give generously and feel every flicker of disconnection, so you offer more instead of letting yourself be received.

"I want to be received without leading it all."
