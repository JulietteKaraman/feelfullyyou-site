About

Juliette Karaman-van Schaardenburg

I bring the va-va-voom back into relationships that have gone quiet.

For twenty-seven years I've worked with couples, women, and men on what happens in the body when love stays but the spark stops moving. Fifteen of those years specifically in couples and trauma work, where the body holds what words cannot reach, and touch becomes the language of restoration.

My clients are couples who love each other and have lost the language to reach each other. Women returning to their own sensation, learning to read what their body is doing before their mind decides. Men learning to read what their partner is doing before their mind catches up.

I built these frameworks from my own journey, not from a textbook.
