HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. Fifteen minutes.

01

Pick a card

Person A picks. Person B reads it aloud. That small act of one person reading to the other already changes the air in the room.

02

One answer

Person A answers from the heart. No interrupting. No fixing. No debating. Person B holds three responses only: Thank you. Clarify that. Summarise that.

03

Switch

Same card. Same question. Person B answers now. 15 minutes per card, going deeper with each turn. Then switch to a new card whenever you are ready.

ABOUT JULIETTE KARAMAN

I bring the va-va-voom back into relationships that have stopped moving.

For twenty-seven years I have worked with couples, women, and men on what happens in the body and the emotions when love stays but the heat fades. The way back is structure. Structured touch, structured communication, structured dates.

I built these cards because I needed the tool first. Fifteen years in the room with couples who love each other and cannot reach each other. I work with the body and the emotions together. The patterns couples cannot see are carried in both. The right question opens what years of talking around it could not.
