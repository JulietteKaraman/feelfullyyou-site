None. Cancel with 2 days notice after your first three months by emailing support@feelfullyyou.com. That's it.

No. I'm not your therapist and this isn't a therapeutic relationship. It's coaching, embodiment work, and translation, helping you understand what's running underneath, and giving you something specific to do about it.

Not unless you choose to share it. No name required, no profile photo. Anonymous submissions, anonymous group presence if you want it. What happens in the group stays there.

Roughly 30–45 minutes a week if you engage with everything. The Monday audio, the Thursday guided practice, and Friday's riff. The live session is once a month.
