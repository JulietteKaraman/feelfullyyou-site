Meet the Founder & CEO

Juliette Karaman-van Schaardenburg

Speaker | Mentor | Self-Relationship Expert | Podcast Host

Juliette Karaman is a global voice in intimacy, self-leadership, and somatic reclamation.

With over 27 years of experience, she supports high-achieving leaders, couples, and visionaries to reignite intimacy, rebuild self-trust, and reconnect with desire, not through effort, but through embodied safety.

At the heart of her work is Touch Base®, her signature methodology that combines hypnotherapy, EMDR, trauma integration, energetic attunement, and sensual reawakening. Through this somatic framework, clients dissolve shame, release held emotion, and rewire their relationship to power, pleasure, and presence.

Juliette is the creator of the Rapid Release Rewire & Restore Method® and the host of The Scrumptious Woman® podcast (Top 2.5% worldwide). Her work ranges from private mentorship to luxury retreats, from whispered voice notes to packed keynote rooms.

Known for her outrageous laugh and unfiltered presence, Juliette bridges the worlds of deep healing and delicious aliveness, helping clients and audiences alike remember who they were before the world told them who to be.

Keynote // Topics

What Juliette speaks on.

Juliette brings honesty, depth, and aliveness to every stage she graces. Her sessions are experiential, trauma-informed, and infused with lived wisdom, leaving audiences transformed, not just inspired.

01

Touch Base®: The Missing Link to Intimacy, Safety & Self-Trust

True intimacy doesn't start with desire, it begins with safety.

Discover how the Touch Base® method rewires relational patterns and restores embodied trust. A keynote that demystifies emotional intimacy and gives audiences a felt sense of how connection actually lands.

02

When She Pulls Away: Unspoken Trauma & the Path to True Safety

Why intimacy strains when trauma is unspoken, and how to meet it with presence, not pressure.

Ideal for couples, therapists, and anyone longing to deepen connection, this talk offers powerful reframes and tools to meet disconnection with attunement.

03

Healing Shame Through Sensual Empowerment

For the woman who's done the work, but still doesn't feel free in her body.

This keynote invites participants into a radically new relationship with their body, where pleasure isn't performance, but permission. Juliette shares real-world tools to unwind shame, awaken sensation, and reclaim your erotic wholeness.

04

Life After Loss: How Grief Becomes the Gateway to Joy

Divorce, death, rupture, this is not the end.

With deep compassion and lived experience, Juliette guides audiences through how grief, identity shifts, and life transitions can be portals to embodiment, joy, and radical self-trust.

05

She Wants to Want You: Why Women Micromanage Intimacy & How to Shift It

For the woman who longs to receive but can't stop controlling.

This talk explores how over-functioning kills desire, and how to move from micromanaging into surrender. For women, couples, and anyone ready to reclaim intimacy that feels alive.
