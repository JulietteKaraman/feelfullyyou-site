061 Conversations on Life, Love, and Facing Mortality

Hosted by Juliette Karaman · 6 February 2024

Apple Podcasts Spotify All episodes

Hello, my loves! Juliette here, to  welcome you back to another heart-to-heart on The Scrumptious Woman podcast. Today, we dive into the symphony of life, exploring relationships, healing journeys, and the profound art of letting go.

Summary
In this episode, I shares insights from my unique role as a death doula, guiding individuals through life's transitions. I reflects on the beauty of maintaining relationships, drawing from personal experiences of friendship with my ex-husband and co-parenting our four children.

Key Takeaways:

  1. Relationship Building: Juliette emphasises the importance of genuine connections in an age dominated by social media. She encourages listeners to cherish the basic human needs of being seen, heard, understood, and loved.
  2. Life and Death Conversations: The episode delves into Juliette's experiences assisting those facing life-threatening illnesses. Explore the emotional challenges, the power of presence, and the significance of slowing down in the face of mortality.
  3. Healing Journey: Discover Juliette's holistic approach to healing, incorporating hypnosis, energetic healing, and spinal attunement. Uncover the value of releasing the constant search for a cure and allowing the body, mind, and soul to return to a state of peace.
  4. Preparing for Death: Juliette reflects on the honour and complexity of helping someone prepare for their death. Explore forgiveness, resolutions, and the deep introspection that comes with facing the end of life.
  5. Reflecting on Life: Listeners are encouraged to ponder their own relationships, slow down, and honour every connection. Juliette discusses the importance of acknowledging regrets, finding peace, and embracing a scrumptious approach to life.

Stay scrumptious, my loves, and remember to treasure each note in your life's beautiful melody.
